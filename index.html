Privacy Policy

Your privacy is strictly respected. Here is how DARTV handles your data:

  • Local Processing: All playlist files (.m3u, .json, .txt) that you upload or sync are processed entirely locally within your web browser. We do not store your personal media lists on external servers.
  • No Tracking: DARTV does not use cookies to track your viewing habits or inject third-party advertising trackers into your session.
  • Browser Extensions: For a seamless mobile viewing experience, we recommend using third-party browser extensions (like CORS Unblocker or Kiwi Browser). Please refer to their respective privacy policies regarding how they handle network requests.

If you have any privacy concerns, please contact Dar Ajaz directly via the details on the About Us page.
